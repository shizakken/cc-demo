Our Story

Started with a bag of beans and a big idea.

Grounds & Co. opened in 2018 in a 400 sq ft space with one espresso machine, two bar stools, and a firm belief that good coffee could anchor a neighborhood.

Today we roast our own beans, train baristas from around the city, and host weekly cuppings open to anyone curious enough to show up.

We're not a chain. We're your corner coffee shop — just doing it really, really well.
